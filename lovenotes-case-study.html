The Research

Small Daily Moments Matter More Than Big Conversations

The Science of Appreciation (Gottman Institute)

Regular gratitude and admiration predict staying together. Lack of appreciation predicts divorce more than conflict. "Turning toward" partner's bids for connection has 94% accuracy in predicting relationship success.

Qualitative Research

Analyzed 2,000+ Reddit posts to understand relationship pain points. Top pattern: Trust erosion from small disconnections. Couples follow a path of avoidance → silent withdrawal → crisis. Without consistent low-friction rituals, couples drift.

Why Existing Solutions Fail

Lifestyle/wellness apps have 2-8% 30-day retention. Push notifications get 4-15% open rates. Apps require downloads, logins, and constant engagement. SMS delivers 98% open rates with no friction.

The Solution

SMS-First Micro-Rituals

LoveNotes reframes relationship health as a habit-formation challenge, not a software problem. Software should support human connection, not replace it.

1
Daily SMS Prompt

Each partner receives a science-backed reflection prompt via text message. Questions are grounded in Gottman research and designed to build appreciation.

2
Private Exchange

Partners see each other's responses directly—no AI interpretation, no algorithmic filtering. Your words, unmodified, delivered to your partner.

3
Weekly LoveLetter

Every Sunday, couples receive a compiled reflection of their week together. A moment to pause, reflect, and appreciate the connection they've built.

"How can we help love flourish in tiny, consistent ways—without pressure, without demands, without surveillance?"

Technical Execution

Challenges Solved

1
Technical Complexity & Edge Cases

SMS scheduling, state machines, and time zones are fragile. Built comprehensive testing tools, simulated couple logic, and multi-LLM debugging workflows.

2
Data Pairing & AI Safety

Late responses need correct pairing; prevent hallucinations. Implemented structured JSON responses, pairing buffers, and strict prompt engineering.

3
Emotional Safety vs. Engagement

Balance gratitude themes without inducing shame. Created science-backed curriculum with recurring appreciation and habit-focused messaging.

4
Trials & Cost Control

Free trials vs. SMS cost risk. Implemented capped trial volume, value-first pricing, and sustainable cost modeling.

Key Learnings

PM Growth & Insights

1. Research Redirected the Vision

Early assumptions about biometric/AI-driven conflict tools were unrealistic. Research revealed simple prompts are validated and powerful—prevented "cool but not useful" product bloat.

2. Minimalism Became a Strategy, Not a Compromise

Reducing onboarding complexity created trust. Postponing features drove focus. Constraint forced commitment to honoring users' words over AI interpretations.

3. User Feedback Transformed the Product

Users rejected complex workflows and embraced lightweight prompts. Learned to prioritize real value over novelty—the best features are often the simplest ones.

4. Systems Complexity Is Hidden Until You Build

State machines, cron logic, and edge cases created unexpected complexity. SMS is far less flexible than an app—every constraint forced better design decisions.

5. New Opportunities Emerge Through Use

"LoveStory" (Spotify-wrapped style recap) emerged organically from usage patterns. Designing for vulnerability and trust revealed opportunities beyond the initial roadmap.
