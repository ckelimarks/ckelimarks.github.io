About Me

Hi there! I'm Christopher K-Lee Marks, a Founder, Educator, Creative Technologist, and Relationship Coach with a passion for designing tools and experiences that foster human connection, personal growth, and creative expression. I love transforming soulful ideas into tangible products that make a real difference—whether it's through music, apps, coaching, or education.

My journey in creative technology and transformation work began over a decade ago when I built a community recording studio in rural Mexico. Since then, I've worked on a wide range of projects—from AI-driven learning platforms to music licensing and podcasting services, honing my skills in product development, digital storytelling, and emotional intelligence coaching.

I'm always exploring new ways to blend science, spirituality, and design—and I love collaborating on projects that have heart. When I'm not building or teaching, you can find me making music, mentoring creatives, or experimenting with tools that bring people closer to themselves and each other.
