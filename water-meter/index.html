The Real Water Hogs

  1. Crypto mining - absurd
  2. Chip fabrication - TSMC uses 150,000+ tons/day
  3. Video streaming at scale - Netflix alone is ~1% of internet traffic
  4. AI training (not inference) - GPT-4 training estimated at 700,000+ L

Perspective: A single load of laundry uses ~50-100 liters. Your daily Opus habit is roughly equivalent to one extra load of laundry.

The uncomfortable truth: buying a new phone every 2 years matters more than your AI usage.
